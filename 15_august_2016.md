###Grammar
きがつく ~ to have sympathy for, to feel in the same way

＿＿＿てしまう。
Two meanings:
1. Something done completely, or fully.
2. Something done which you now feel really bad about.

eg: 映画を見てしまいました (1)
    さいふをおとしてしまいました (2)

＿＿＿ようにします。
Affixed to verbs when trying really hard to do them.
